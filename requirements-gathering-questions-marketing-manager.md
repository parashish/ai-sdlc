# Preliminary Interview Questions for Marketing Managers in a Car Rental System

## General Questions
1. Can you tell us about your previous marketing experience in the automotive or rental industry?
2. What marketing strategies have you found most effective for attracting customers to a car rental service?
3. How do you stay informed about the latest trends in the car rental industry?

## Customer Engagement
4. What methods do you use to identify and understand the target audience for a car rental business?
5. How would you approach building customer loyalty and retaining repeat clients in the car rental industry?
6. In your opinion, what role does customer feedback play in shaping marketing strategies?

## Digital Marketing
7. What online marketing tactics do you believe are essential for car rental companies?
8. How do you measure the success of digital marketing campaigns? Can you provide an example?
9. What platforms do you think are the most effective for advertising car rental services?

## Collaboration and Teamwork
10. How do you work with other departments, such as sales and customer service, to align marketing efforts?
11. Can you describe a time when you led a marketing project? What were the challenges and outcomes?

## Adaptability and Innovation
12. How do you incorporate new technologies or tools in your marketing strategies?
13. What innovative marketing methods do you think could be beneficial for car rental businesses?
