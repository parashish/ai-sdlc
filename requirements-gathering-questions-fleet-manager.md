# Preliminary Interview Questions for Fleet Managers

## General Questions
1. Can you describe your role as a fleet manager?
2. What types of vehicles are currently in your fleet?
3. How do you determine the optimal number of vehicles needed for your operations?

## Maintenance and Operations
4. What is your process for regular vehicle maintenance?
5. How do you track vehicle usage and performance?
6. Are there any challenges you face with vehicle downtime or maintenance?

## Technology and Systems
7. What software or tools do you currently use for fleet management?
8. How do you manage vehicle tracking and real-time data?

## Customer Experience
9. How do you ensure customer satisfaction with the fleet?
10. What metrics do you consider important for measuring fleet performance from a customer perspective?
