# Requirements Gathering Questions for Finance and Accounting Managers in a Car Rental System

## General Questions
1. What are the key financial metrics that you track regularly?
2. How do you currently manage financial reporting and analysis?

## Budgeting and Forecasting
3. What is your process for budgeting and forecasting?  
4. How frequently do you revisit and adjust your budgets?  

## Pricing and Revenue
5. How do you determine the pricing structure for rentals?  
6. What factors most greatly influence your pricing decisions? 

## Expense Management
7. How do you manage and allocate operational expenses?  
8. What types of financial controls do you have in place to ensure accuracy in expense reporting?

## Compliance and Regulations
9. What regulatory requirements must your financial practices adhere to?  
10. How do you ensure compliance with these regulations?

## Financial Systems and Tools
11. What financial software or systems are you currently using?  
12. Are there any limitations or challenges you face with your current systems?

## Reporting and Analysis
13. What types of financial reports do you regularly generate?  
14. How do you analyze financial data for decision-making?

## Other Considerations
15. Are there any specific financial challenges or pain points you are currently facing?  
16. What features would you like to see in a new financial management tool?
