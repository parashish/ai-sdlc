# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for Marketing Manager

## Overview 
This document outlines the requirements for the Marketing Manager role in the car rental system. The aim is to define the duties, responsibilities, and expectations for the marketing manager to facilitate the promotion, brand awareness, and customer acquisition for the car rental service.

## Goals  
1. Increase brand awareness and customer engagement.  
2. Develop and implement strategic marketing plans.  
3. Analyze market trends to inform marketing strategies.  
4. Collaborate with sales and product teams to align marketing strategies with business objectives.

## Roles and Responsibilities  
- Develop marketing strategies tailored to target audiences.  
- Manage and optimize marketing campaigns across multiple channels (social media, email, website, etc.).  
- Collaborate with the finance team to establish budgets for marketing initiatives.  
- Analyze campaign performance metrics to improve future efforts.  
- Conduct market research to identify new opportunities and threats.

## Key Features  
1. **Customer Insights**: Implement tools to gather customer feedback and preferences to inform marketing strategies.  
2. **Campaign Management Dashboard**: A centralized platform to monitor and manage all marketing campaigns.  
3. **Performance Analytics**: Integration with analytics tools to track and report on the performance of marketing initiatives.

## Evaluation Metrics  
1. Increase in website traffic.  
2. Growth in social media engagement (likes, shares, comments).  
3. Conversion rate from leads to customers.

## Budget Considerations  
- Allocate funds for advertising, tools, and potential personnel expansion based on performance metrics and goals. 

## Conclusion  
This PRD serves as the foundation for all marketing activities in the car rental system. The marketing manager is expected to drive initiatives that lead to increased utilization of the car rental service and customer satisfaction.
