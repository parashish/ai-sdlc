# Preliminary Interview Questions for Business Analysts in a Car Rental System

## General Questions
1. **Experience and Background**  
   - What is your experience in business analysis, particularly in the transportation or rental industry?
   - Can you provide examples of projects you've worked on related to car rentals?

2. **Understanding Business Needs**  
   - How do you determine the business requirements for a new car rental system?
   - What stakeholders would you consult when gathering requirements for this system?

## Functional Requirements
3. **Booking Process**  
   - What key features should the booking process include?  
   - How should the system handle booking modifications and cancellations?

4. **Pricing and Payment**  
   - What pricing models do you think are most effective in the car rental industry?
   - What payment methods should the system support?

## Reporting and Analytics
5. **Data Usage**  
   - What types of reports should the system generate for management?  
   - How would you track the performance of the rental business through data analytics?

## User Experience
6. **Customer Experience**  
   - What aspects of the user interface are most critical for customers booking a car?
   - How would you ensure that the system is user-friendly?

## Compliance and Regulations
7. **Legal Considerations**  
   - What regulatory requirements must be considered in a car rental system?
   - How would you ensure compliance with data protection laws related to customer information?

## Miscellaneous
8. **Future Enhancements**  
   - What future trends do you see impacting the car rental industry, and how should the system accommodate them?  
   - How important is integrating technology like mobile applications or AI in the rental process?  

## Closing  
9. **Final Thoughts**  
   - Do you have any questions about our organization or this particular project?  
   - What is your vision for a successful car rental system?
